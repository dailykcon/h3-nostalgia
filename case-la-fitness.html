Case Study / Product Design

LA Fitness Mobile Redesign

A cleaner member experience for finding classes, managing check-ins, and making everyday fitness tasks feel less fragmented.
Role
UX/UI Design
Focus
Mobile IA, booking flows, usability
Timeline
Concept case study

Overview

Making routine gym tasks easier to complete.

The redesign explores how a large fitness app could better support repeat visits: faster class discovery, clearer location context, simpler account actions, and a visual system that feels more modern without hiding the practical tools members need most.

Class Schedule
Club Check-In
Member Home

Prioritized repeat actions

Home and schedule flows emphasize the jobs members repeat most often: checking in, browsing classes, and managing their club context.

Clarified decision states

Class cards expose availability, time, instructor, location, and booking state so users can make a decision without extra digging.

Modernized the system

The visual direction keeps the brand practical, but introduces stronger hierarchy, calmer surfaces, and more consistent components.
